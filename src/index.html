Causes of injury and illness

  • Body stressing (strains, sprains, pulled muscles etc.) - 38%
  • Falls, trips and slips - 24%
  • Being hit by moving objects - 16%
  • Hitting objects with a part of the body - 8%
  • Mental stress - 6%
  • Other - 8%

Types of injury and illness

  • Traumatic joint/ligament and muscle/tendon injuries - 41%
  • Wounds, lacerations, amputations and internal organ damage - 16%
  • Musculoskeletal and connective tissue diseases - 16%
  • Fractures - 11%
  • Mental disorders - 7%
  • Other - 9%

Injury and illness claims by occupation

  • Labourers - 25%
  • Technicians and trade workers - 19%
  • Community and personal service workers - 17%
  • Machinery operators and drivers - 14%
  • Professionals - 10%
  • Sales workers - 5%
  • Managers - 5%
  • Clerical and administrative workers - 4%
